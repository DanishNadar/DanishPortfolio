# Background semantics

The animated background is not meant to be random decoration. It is designed around Danish's AI engineering brand:

- **Neural grid:** represents model reasoning, AI systems, and decision graphs.
- **Autonomy lanes:** represents path planning, autonomous vehicle work, and safer transportation systems.
- **Sensor-fusion radar rings:** represents robotics perception, camera/radar/LiDAR-style thinking, and physical-world AI.
- **Data beams:** represents signals moving between software, hardware, and people.
- **Blue/cyan/red palette:** blue/cyan signals engineering clarity and perception; red adds urgency, impact, and the safety mission behind autonomy.

The motion should stay subtle: it supports the brand without becoming the focus of the page.

- **Holographic electric SUV silhouette:** represents the safer-road mission behind autonomy work. It is Cadillac Lyriq-inspired in shape without using brand marks, and it connects the background motion to vehicle perception, planning, and validation.
- **Linux-first tooling layer:** terminal, automation, and repeatable development are now part of the portfolio story because the projects are built through Linux-oriented workflows, not only visual demos.
